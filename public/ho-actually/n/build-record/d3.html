build record

definition
depth 2/2 − deeper + surface

A running, add-only list at the bottom of the build plan recording what each step actually finished and where the build stands.

The append-only log grafted onto the tail of the K4 ho overview: one entry per ho close and per replan checkpoint, each shaped as a state-summary block, never rewritten. Cold and forward-only — the human-facing, canonical ledger of what a build actually did, the counterpart to the hot working-memory handoff.
