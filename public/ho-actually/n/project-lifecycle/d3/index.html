project lifecycle

definition
depth 2/3 − deeper + surface

A one-word label for how far along a project is, from planning to shipped.

Where the thing being built sits in its life: kamae (pre-build, still framing), dev (building, not yet user-exercised), beta (real users or the practitioner driving it, hardening), production (shipped, in use). The fourth field of the state-summary block. Orthogonal to the practitioner's shu-ha-ri stage and to the stage: Kamae field — and deliberately not named "status," which belongs to a document's own status: frontmatter state.
