forward-only

concept
depth 2/3 − deeper + surface

Finished work stays finished; a mistake is answered with a new document, never by editing the old one.

Closed hos stay closed; closed decisions stay on the record. The response to superseded work is a new document that names what it supersedes — never a reopening, rewrite, or retroactive edit. Typographical fixes are the only exception. Governs the frozen and sealed mutability regimes; living is the declared exception.
