mutability (living / frozen / sealed)

concept
depth 2/3 − deeper + surface

The scale of how much an artifact may change over its life — living (revised freely), frozen (changed only by override), or sealed (never again).

The axis naming how an artifact changes over its life. living — revised in place (README, overview, seed by dated revision, the Basis of Design). frozen — not edited in place, changed only by a superseding addendum (system design); thaw-able through the addendum mechanism, still governs the build. sealed — final, never changes (closed hos, sidequests, addenda, dogfood findings, devlogs/Reflect, executed ATs, propagation-ledger commits); a future document may respond but nothing supersedes it in force. Forward-only governs frozen and sealed; living is the declared exception.
