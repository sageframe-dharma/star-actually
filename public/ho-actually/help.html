about

Ho System, Actually is an instance of *, Actually — knowledge structured as a navigable graph of concepts instead of a document you scroll.

Every concept is a node. Every node has depth layers: the name, the definition, the usage, the relationships, the theory. Dial in as deep as you need. Stop when understanding clicks.

The keys

  • − — dial down: deeper into this concept
  • + — dial up: back toward the surface
  • ↓ — onward: where this concept leads
  • ↑ — back along your path, to the depth you left
  • → — branch: a lateral detour
  • ↵ — return from a branch to exactly where you left
  • / — search everything
  • ? — this screen

The ideas

Your path is always visible. Branches return in one keypress. Getting lost is not possible.

The question box maps what you're wondering to the right node at the right depth. The AI is the receptionist, not the doctor — it walks you to the room and leaves.
